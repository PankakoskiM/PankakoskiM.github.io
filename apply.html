The Pool

The Pool is a group of top AI talent who work as freelancers on artificial intelligence projects as part of the core team at LABRA.AI. Every project has a Project Manager who will be the go-between the team and the client, so you can focus on building great products.

If accepted into the pool, you will work as a freelancer and you will be selected for different projects for enterprise clients based on your skill set and project requirements. Depending on your predetermined availability, you will work with the core LABRA.AI team 1-7 days a week in Otaniemi during a project.

The Pool is an excellent opportunity to work on practical AI projects, develop your skills and earn excellent compensation for your efforts. Compensation will be based on either an hourly rate or a percentage of the project revenue.

Ideal candidates are stellar, proven engineers educated in machine learning or deep learning with deep personal interest in artificial intelligence. You should be excited about delivering beautiful, maintainable, bug-free code to spec, and meeting or beating deadlines.
