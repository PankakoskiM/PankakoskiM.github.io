Project Flow

  • Project Scope

    A process within the client company is selected for the project together with the customer. The process is selected based on three criteria: the client's business goals, the suitability of artificial intelligence for the process and the availability of data.

  • Solution Prototyping

    Various solutions are prototyped using data from the selected process. This stage lasts several days, at the end of which the best solutions are presented to the client.

  • Project Plan

    The project plan for the final solution is formalized based on solution prototyping.

  • Solution Deployment

    The artificial intelligence software is built and deployed. This stage is expected to last approximately a month, however it depends on the scope of the project.
